About Us

"An online Sabalu market for small businesses."
The Local Hut began with the humble idea of creating an online hub for small businesses to reach a wider audience of locals from the Northern Mariana Islands.
The Problem
Most small businesses are ran by entrepreneurs who manage their businesses at home. Occassionally, you may find these businesses having pop-ups at local markets, festivals, and events. However, with no physical location firmly established, it is difficult for the average population to know that these businesses exist other than word of mouth.
The Solution
The Local Hut is a one-stop marketing solution for all small businesses in the Northern Mariana Islands.
Our Future Plans
As we grow, we hope to expand to different businesses categories including art, agriculture, digital services, retail, and etc. with the requirement that you must be a small business! If you are interested in being a part of this journey, please contact us.
